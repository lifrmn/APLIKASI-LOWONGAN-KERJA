# Privacy Policy — Sulbar Kerja

**Effective date:** July 1, 2026
**Data controller:** Manpower Office of West Sulawesi Province (Disnaker Sulbar)
**Contact:** support@sulbarkerja.id
**Data Protection Officer:** dpo@sulbarkerja.id

Sulbar Kerja ("the App") is the official digital job marketplace operated by the Government of West Sulawesi Province, Indonesia. This policy explains how we collect, use, store and protect your personal data in accordance with Indonesian Law No. 27 of 2022 on Personal Data Protection ("PDP Law").

## 1. Data we collect
- **Account & profile:** full name, email, phone, hashed password, optional username & avatar.
- **Job seeker profile (optional):** NIK (masked at rest and in responses), place & date of birth, gender, address, region hierarchy, education, work experience, skills, certificates, portfolio, CV, e-KTP (identity card) image.
- **Company account:** company name, business field, contact info, logo, legal documents.
- **Application activity:** applications submitted, status changes, chat with HR.
- **Technical:** IP address, User-Agent, device basics, audit logs for sensitive actions.

We do **not** collect precise GPS location without your explicit consent.

## 2. Purposes
1. Provide job marketplace services and matching.
2. Verify identity and company legitimacy to prevent fraud.
3. Aggregate labor statistics for public policy (never identifying individuals).
4. Notify you about application status, interviews, announcements.
5. Security (login anomaly detection, rate limiting, audit).
6. Legal obligations.

We do **not** sell your personal data.

## 3. Legal basis
- Your consent at signup (Art. 20(a) PDP Law).
- Public interest / government task performed by Disnaker Sulbar (Art. 20(f)).
- Compliance with legal obligations (Art. 20(c)).

## 4. Sharing with third parties
- Employers you apply to (only data relevant to your application).
- Infrastructure providers (cloud hosting, transactional email) bound by data processing agreements.
- Authorities upon lawful order.

We do **not** share data with advertisers or data brokers.

## 5. Sensitive data — NIK & e-KTP
- Uploading the national ID card (e-KTP) is optional.
- NIK is displayed masked (e.g. `7604********0001`) except to the owner and admins holding the `sensitive.identity.read` permission.
- e-KTP files are not publicly accessible; downloads require authentication and are logged.

## 6. Retention

| Data type | Retention |
|---|---|
| Active accounts | While account is active |
| Inactive accounts > 24 months | Automatically anonymized |
| Application records | ≥ 3 years (statistics & legal) |
| Audit logs | ≥ 2 years |
| e-KTP files | 12 months after last verification, unless earlier deletion requested |

## 7. Security
- Passwords hashed with bcrypt.
- HTTPS/TLS in transit.
- Refresh tokens stored as hashes with family-based rotation.
- Sensitive files served only via authenticated endpoints with audit logging.
- Rate limiting and least-privilege RBAC.

We commit to notifying you within **72 hours** of any incident affecting your personal data, in line with the PDP Law.

## 8. Your rights
Access, correction, deletion, consent withdrawal, restriction, objection, portability, and complaint to the supervisory authority. Contact **support@sulbarkerja.id**; we respond within 14 business days.

## 9. Children
The App is intended for users **aged 15 and above** (working-age threshold per Statistics Indonesia). Younger users must have parental supervision.

## 10. Changes
Material changes will be announced in-app and/or by email at least 7 days before taking effect.
